Welcome to the Dancers Hand Book!

In this page you will learn how to survive through your first dance class.

Follow these top 10 tips for your first dance class:

1. Be on time!

2.Wear the correct unifrom (e.g. bun and leotard for ballet)

3. Never yawn or look at the clock!

4. Make sure that your dance bag is packed with EVERYTHING, from a tennis ball to a water bottle

5. Do NOT wear jewellery

6. NEVER EVER weal heals on a spring loaded floor or into a dance class

7. Pay attention at all time

8. Always give 11%

9. Never talk while the teacher is instructing or demonstrating

10. ALWAYS take corrections on board even if you disagree
